Quests are an important source of gems, speedups and resources. If you want to jump up those leaderboards you need to complete as many quests as possible.

All quests are 55 minutes long starting at five past the hour.

Quests repeat every 11 hours. Some quests such as troop training and monster slaying occur more often than others.

To earn points in a quest you need to finish an activity within the quest period. Starting gather resources, construction, research or train troops before the quest starts is a good strategy to get an edge.

You are only competing against other users in your realm and in your bracket range. The brackets are based on Town Hall level.

  • TH 3–6
  • TH 7–9
  • TH 10–12
  • TH 13–15
  • TH 16–18
  • TH 19–22
  • TH 23–25

Basic rewards are on offer for one star, two star or three stars on each quest. These are important and can help build the gems / speedups you need to top the quest leaderboards.

The best rewards are given for coming top 3 on the leaderboards. You should always aim to hit 1st on a quest every so often. Make sure you have enough resources / gems / speedups before you start!
